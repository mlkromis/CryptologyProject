SHA-3

The Secure Hash Algorithm is a family of cryptographic hash functions published by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) as a U.S. Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPS) (siehe Wikipedia). The NIST had chosen the hash function Keccak as SHA-3 and thus successor of the SHA-1/2 family.

A few candidates have achieved the second round of the selection process. JCrypTool implements the following candidates:

  • ECHO
  • JH
  • Skein

More information is available on the sub sites.
